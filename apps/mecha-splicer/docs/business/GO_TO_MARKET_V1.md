# Mecha-Splicer Go-To-Market V1

## Goal
Reach first recurring revenue with one tightly-scoped offer in 14 days.

## Core Offer (V1)
`Printable Production Fixtures Pack`

What is included:
- 3 production-ready fixture templates (alignment jig, repeat placement plate, modular clamp base)
- Parametric source (`.scad`) + pre-rendered `.stl`
- `ASSEMBLY.scad`, `PRINT_PLAN.md`, `PARTS.json`, `bom.csv`
- Quick-start guide with "first print in <2 hours" flow

Who it is for:
- Small makers using desktop 3D printers + laser/engraving/cutting workflows
- Users who need repeatable placement and speed, not aesthetic product shells

Who it is not for:
- High-precision industrial tooling (tight tolerance contracts)
- Buyers expecting full simulation/FEA certification

## Channels (No Manual Outreach)
- Primary: Gumroad (fast iteration, simple digital delivery)
- Secondary: Etsy (high buyer intent for ready-made jig products)
- Discovery support: Printables/other maker platform free teaser models pointing to paid pack

## 14-Day Execution
1. Days 1-3: Lock V1 scope and generate pack assets
2. Days 4-5: Create listing assets (screenshots, demo video, before/after workflow)
3. Days 6-7: Publish Gumroad + Etsy listing
4. Days 8-14: Run A/B listing copy and pricing tests; collect support and conversion data

## Success Criteria (V1)
- At least 1 paid conversion in first 14 days
- <20 minutes average support time per buyer
- At least 2 repeatable buyer-reported wins (time saved / setup simplification)

## Failure Criteria
- Zero conversions with >300 targeted listing views
- High support burden (>60 minutes per buyer)

If failure criteria hit, pivot offer packaging and positioning before adding new features.
