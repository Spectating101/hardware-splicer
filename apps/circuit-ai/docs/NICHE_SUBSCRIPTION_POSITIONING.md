# Circuit-AI Niche Subscription Positioning

## Positioning Statement

Circuit-AI is a **KiCad respin-prevention and manufacturing-handoff automation layer** for freelancers and small hardware teams shipping iterative PCB revisions.

It is not positioned as a generic "AI electronics assistant for everyone."

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

- Freelance PCB/KiCad engineers with repeated client revisions
- Small hardware teams (1-8 engineers) moving from prototype to manufacturing package
- Agencies that deliver PCB+firmware drafts and need repeatable QA/handoff outputs

## Core Buyer Pain

- Revisions are slow because every round re-checks the same things manually
- Manufacturing package prep is inconsistent (BOM/Gerber/package handoff drift)
- Client constraints are often underspecified, causing avoidable errors and back-and-forth

## Core Promise

1. Faster revision loops with structured intake and explicit open questions
2. Quantitative validation outputs (not vague warnings)
3. Repeatable handoff package generation for manufacturing

## Why Subscription (instead of one-time)

Subscription is justified when users run recurring revision cycles:
- multiple boards per month,
- multiple iterations per board,
- repeated preflight + package generation.

For one-off boards, fixed-price service or prepaid credits are usually a better fit.

## Offer Narrative (What to Say)

"Circuit-AI reduces revision friction in KiCad workflows. You still make engineering decisions; the platform standardizes intake, validation, and manufacturing handoff so each iteration is faster and more defensible."

## Anti-Overpromise Guardrails

Do not sell this as:
- fully autonomous final PCB design from vague prompts,
- compliance certification replacement,
- guaranteed performance in specialized RF/high-speed domains without explicit constraints.

Sell this as:
- an engineering workflow accelerator with clear scope and limits.

## Suggested Niche Pricing (Starting Point)

- Builder: USD 29/month (solo recurring usage)
- Pro: USD 79/month (freelancer with active client workloads)
- Team: USD 199/month (small team, shared usage)

## Conversion Trigger

Move a buyer from service/credits to subscription only when:
- they have recurring monthly revision workload,
- they use the workflow at least weekly,
- they benefit from fast repeatable preflight/handoff rather than one-off support.
