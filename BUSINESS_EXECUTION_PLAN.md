**Date:** Jan 16, 2026
**Status:** Verified & Validated

## 1. The Core Strategy
We have pivoted from a generic "SaaS" model to a **"Hybrid Infrastructure"** model. We monetize through three distinct channels, ordered by speed to cash.

---

## 2. Channel 1: The "Mercenary" Freelancer (Fast Cash)
**Concept:** Use the software's speed advantage to crush manual competitors on freelance markets.

*   **The Service:** "PDF Schematic to KiCad Conversion" & "PCB Reverse Engineering."
*   **The Market:** Upwork / Fiverr.
*   **The Rate:** $100 - $500 per job.
*   **The Edge:** Circuit-AI's `GenerativeAgent` turns hours of manual drafting into minutes of review.
*   **Validation:** Verified demand for PCB design/conversion jobs (2025/2026 data).

*   **The Targets:**

*   **The Asset:** `GRANT_PROPOSAL_TEMPLATE.md` is ready to submit.

## 4. Channel 3: The "Micro-Factory" (Long-Term Wealth)
**Concept:** Arbitrage and Upcycling of e-waste using the robotic platform.

*   **The Strategy:** Source -> Refurbish -> Sell.
*   **Validated Niches (Taiwan/Indonesia):**
    *   **Gogoro/Scooter Controllers:** High scrap availability, high resale value ($100+).
    *   **Broken GPUs:** High liquidity, moderate repair difficulty ($60+ margin).
    *   **Industrial PLCs:** High reliability value ($80+ margin).
*   **The Tool:** The "Manual Guide Mode" (AR) allows human operators (you) to perform repairs efficiently before full robotic automation.
